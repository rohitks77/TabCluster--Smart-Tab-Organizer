How It Works

Get organized in seconds

1

Install the Extension

Add Auto Group Tabs to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store or load it in developer mode.

2

Click the Icon

Click the extension icon in your toolbar to open the popup interface.

3

Group Your Tabs

Click "Group" to instantly organize all tabs by domain into color-coded groups.

4

Customize & Enjoy

Configure settings, create custom rules, and let auto-grouping handle new tabs.
